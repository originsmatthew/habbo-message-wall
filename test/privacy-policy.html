Privacy Policy

At Habbo Origins Wall of Kindness, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

What Information Do We Collect?

We collect the following types of information:

  • Habbo Names: We collect the name of the Habbo who submits the message and the Habbo name of the recipient.
  • Messages: The kind message you submit is stored for approval and display on the Wall of Kindness.

How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information you provide solely for the purpose of displaying kind messages on the Wall of Kindness. The information is not shared with third parties and is used in accordance with the terms outlined here.

Your Rights

If you wish to have your message removed or wish to correct any details, you may contact us through the form provided on the website, and we will handle your request in a timely manner.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
